Frequently asked questions

What is Twixio, exactly?

Twixio is your AI-powered content sidekick — it helps you create engaging social media posts in seconds, optimized for reach, tone, and platform.

How does Twixio actually help me with content creation?

Twixio uses smart algorithms to write captions, generate ideas, and even suggest hashtags — saving you hours of time and boosting your content performance.

Will it work for all social media platforms?

Yes! Twixio adapts your content to suit Instagram, LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Facebook, and more. Each post is adjusted for the platform’s tone, length, and format.

Can I customize the AI-generated posts?

Absolutely. All generated content can be edited instantly within the app — so you keep full creative control.

Does it include trending keywords and hashtags?

Yes, Twixio tracks what’s hot and weaves trending keywords and hashtags into your content to maximize engagement.

Are the posts SEO-friendly?

Definitely. Twixio crafts copy with built-in SEO structure to help your content get found and rank higher in search.

Can I schedule content directly from Twixio?

Coming soon! We’re working on native scheduling so you can go from idea to post without leaving the platform.

Which languages does Twixio support?

Twixio works in 20+ languages and can match formal, casual, or even brand-specific tones across all major global markets.

Is my data safe with Twixio?

Yes. Your content and information are encrypted and protected with top-tier security protocols. Your data stays yours.
